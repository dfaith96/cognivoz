Product and Service Features

Built for thoughtful emotional support.

Cognivoz is designed to support reflection, self-awareness, coping preparation, and better communication with care professionals.

U

Emotional Understanding

Identifies the feelings, situations, repeated thoughts, and stress triggers that may be present in a user’s message.

  • Summarizes emotional context clearly.
  • Highlights patterns across repeated reflections.
  • Helps users name what they are experiencing.
R

Reasoned Reflection

Uses structured reasoning to help users examine thoughts, assumptions, needs, boundaries, and possible next steps.

  • Encourages balanced thinking.
  • Separates facts, feelings, and interpretations.
  • Offers gentle questions instead of hard conclusions.
G

Guided Coping Tools

Provides grounding exercises, journaling prompts, breathing reminders, and coping plans for stressful moments.

  • Supports practical self-regulation.
  • Offers short prompts for overwhelmed users.
  • Helps prepare for difficult conversations.
T

Therapist Conversation Prep

Helps users organize what to discuss with a licensed therapist, counselor, doctor, or trusted support person.

  • Turns reflections into appointment notes.
  • Lists concerns, goals, and questions.
  • Reduces the pressure of remembering everything.
S

Safety-Aware Boundaries

Recognizes moments where AI support is not enough and encourages professional, urgent, or trusted human help.

  • Uses clear crisis guidance.
  • Avoids diagnosis and medical claims.
  • Keeps support within appropriate limits.

Helps users track mood, stressors, coping strategies, and personal wins over time.

How Cognivoz Works

From emotional signal to supportive clarity.

Cognivoz is built around a careful flow: listen, understand, reason, and suggest safe next steps.

01

Listen to context

The AI reads the user’s words for situation, emotion, intensity, uncertainty, and the relationship or life context around the issue.

02

Name patterns

It identifies repeated themes such as avoidance, overwhelm, guilt, fear, conflict, burnout, grief, or difficulty setting boundaries.

03

Reason with care

Cognivoz separates facts from interpretations, suggests reflective questions, and avoids pretending to know more than the user shared.

04

Offer next steps

It recommends grounding, journaling, conversation prep, boundary language, or professional support when the situation calls for it.

Important Safety Note

Cognivoz is supportive AI, not a licensed therapist or emergency service.

  • It does not diagnose conditions.
  • It does not replace professional care.
  • It should not be used in emergencies.

When to Seek Human Help

If someone may harm themselves or another person, feels unsafe, is in immediate danger, or is unable to cope, they should contact local emergency services, a crisis hotline, a trusted person, or a licensed professional immediately.

Therapist-Supportive Design

Cognivoz is best used to prepare notes, recognize patterns, practice language, and reflect between sessions. It can help users arrive at therapy with clearer thoughts and better questions.

Privacy-Conscious Use

Users should avoid sharing unnecessary personal details, emergency information, passwords, financial data, or information about another person without permission.
